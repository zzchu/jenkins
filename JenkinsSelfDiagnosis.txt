General troubleshooting:\
\'95	Once there is unexpected failure or sifirst step is to check failed/problematic build's console output and search for error, failed, .etc. Error messages usually will be printed out at the bottom of console output\
\'95	Sometimes, we may find some unusual jenkins system exceptions, especially remote connection exception. In this case, please first check slave node log, like https://wme-jenkins.cisco.com:8443/computer/IMAC-SJC/log to make sure connection between slave and master is normal\
\'95	Don't abort wme-jenkins, it may cause windows file being locked \
\'95	junit timeout exception may caused by machine time difference or jenkins processing delay\
